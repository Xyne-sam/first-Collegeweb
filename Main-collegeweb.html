About the Founder

Founder: Pt. Indu Shekhar Jha

Inspired By: Dr. Rajendra Prasad, the first President of India

Built For: Empower Education in India

Learn more about the founder's inspiring journey.

Vision and Mission

Vision

To maintain virtues, integrity, and intellectual culture while imparting quality education and professional skills, fostering cultural heritage, and blending modernity with traditional values.

Mission

To provide equal educational opportunities, especially for underprivileged and marginalized sections of society, ensuring access to quality education without discrimination.

Academic Evolution

  • 1949: Established Commerce Faculty.
  • 1957: Added Science Faculty.
  • 1960: Introduced Arts Faculty.
  • 1963: Incorporated Law Faculty.

Affiliation and Accreditation

Affiliation: Constituent unit of Patliputra University.

Accreditation: NAAC 'A' grade with a CGPA of 3.10/4.
